# 🥗 Diet & Exercise Planner

A simple web app to plan your **daily diet and exercise routine** for each day of the week, and to **calculate calories and protein** for your meals.

## ✨ Features

- **Weekly diet planner**
  - Predefined meal plan for all 7 days (Monday–Sunday)
  - Each day includes Breakfast, Snacks, Lunch, and Dinner
  - Shows **calories** and **protein** for each food item
  - Daily summary of **total calories, protein, carbs (estimated), and fats (estimated)**

- **Workout planner**
  - Day-wise workout focus (Chest & Triceps, Legs, Back & Biceps, Yoga, HIIT, Shoulders & Abs, Recovery)
  - List of exercises with **sets/reps/duration**
  - Estimated **calories burned** per exercise
  - Summary of total exercises and total calories burned

- **Calorie & protein calculator**
  - Add any custom food with:
    - Name
    - Calories
    - Protein
    - Quantity
  - Dynamic table with per-item and total **calories** and **protein**
  - **Quick-add** buttons for common foods (chicken, eggs, rice, paneer, whey, etc.)
  - Remove individual items or clear all entries

- **UI**
  - Modern dark theme
  - Responsive layout (mobile-friendly)
  - Tab-based navigation (Diet / Exercise / Calculator)
  - Day selector for weekly planning

## 🧱 Tech Stack

- **HTML5**
- **CSS3**
- **Vanilla JavaScript (ES6)**

No external frameworks or libraries are used.
